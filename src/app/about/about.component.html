Plum St. Group

The Plum Street Group is a small, full-services software shop. We cater to small businesses and start-up teams. We specialize in Ruby on Rails back-end systems and Angular front-end apps.

The Plum Street Group was founded by Frank Vinci in 2009. We have remained a small team by choice to allow ourselves to remain more agile. We maintain a network of skilled professionals who are able to assist our clients on an as-needed basis.
